What we look for

The kind of people who thrive at Circa.

01  ·  Ideas

Ideas, not just experience.

The financial world keeps changing. We hire people who can spot what’s moving and bring sensible, well-argued ideas to the table.

02  ·  Depth

Technical depth, plainly explained.

Whether your background is accounting, tax or IT, you can hold a serious conversation with a senior client without hiding behind jargon.

03  ·  Care

Care about doing it well.

Fixed-fee work only works when it’s done properly. We hire people who take pride in tidy, accurate, on-time output.
